What are cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to remember your preferences and help secure your session.

Types of cookies we use

  • Strictly necessary – required to log in and keep your session secure.
  • Preference – remember language and accessibility choices.
  • Analytics – help us understand how SDGRS is used so we can improve it.
