Responsible use

Usage policy

Guidelines on what is allowed and not allowed when using SDGRS.

Scope Acceptable use Unacceptable use Enforcement

Scope

This usage policy applies to all users who access SDGRS, including citizens, employees and authorised partners.

Acceptable use

  • Submit grievances, feedback and related information accurately and in good faith.
  • Use language that is respectful and free of abusive or threatening content.
  • Protect your login credentials and notify us if you suspect misuse.

Unacceptable use

Examples of misuse that are not permitted include:

  • Submitting malicious code, spam or automated scripts to disrupt the service.
  • Sharing confidential information about others without authorisation.
  • Attempting to gain unauthorised access to accounts or systems.

Enforcement

Suspected misuse may result in suspension of access and, where appropriate, referral to relevant authorities.

Misuse reporting will be linked to the SDGRS ticketing flow.
